About MovieTime

MovieTime, Inc. is a Bulgarian media company based in Troyan, Bulgaria. Founded in 2023 by Teodora Yuliyanova in Troyan, Bulgaria, it operates the over-the-top subscription video on-demand service MovieTime brand, which includes original films and television series commissioned or acquired by the company, and third-party content licensed from other distributors.
